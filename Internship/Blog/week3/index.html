WEEK 3

In week three, I think I could get many opportunities of working as professional journalists not as an assistant director. And also I could realize why I like journalistic works.

About personally, It was very fun to go to work place everyday. Gradually the number of works what I can do and tasks what my supervisor entrust me increased. It was really glad to me because I could felt I am getting adapting to this journalistic work. Even if I had to go to work at the very early morning for going to a far place for the interview, it was not nasty work for me. I think what journalists do every week is quite same, but every time they can have a stimulating experience. I mean that even though they interview many times, people who they interview are different and every one has a different personality and experience. Therefore, I do not lose interest in doing this job and everyday I can get a new experience which is quite nice.

About professionally, I have learnt that it is important to be calm and have a flexible idea and mind. It is because sometimes an occurrence you have not expected happens, but if you get into a panic, interviewees have a feeling of anxiety. For example, this week I had a chance directing cameraman which order what kind of shots I want for creating a video, so one day before that day I made a list of shots I need and prepared well. However, after I arrived at the restaurant and started to take shots, the owner of the shop asked me not to take the scene of cooking, however, most of the scene which I have made a plan was at the kitchen. Although my supervisor and cameraman helped me and I could make a video, I was in a panic at that moment.

I thought we do not know what will happen, so journalists have to have a flexible idea and skill of responding quickly to the problem.

In the future, I think I will try to have more confidence with my work and talk with interviewees more. And next week in the last week in my internship, so I try to show the result of work which I have learnt in four weeks.
